Hot cakes

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup caster sugar
  • 2 eggs lightly beaten
  • 60g butter, melted, cooled
  • 1 cup (250ml) milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
  • Extra maple and butter to server

Steps

  1. Step 1: Sift the flour and baking powder into a medium mixing bowl. Stir in the sugar. Combine the eggs, butter, milk and vanilla in a jug. Make a well in the centre of the dry ingredients. Pour in the milk mixture and use a whisk to mix to a smooth batter. Cover with plastic wrap. Stand 10 minutes.
  2. Step 2: Heat a medium non-stick frypan over medium to low heat. Lightly oil with cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter into the pan and cook for 2 minutes or until small bubbles appear on the surface. Turn over and cook for 1 minute or until golden and cooked through. Remove from pan and keep warm. Repeat with remaining mixture.
  3. Step 3: Serve hot cakes with extra butter and maple syrup
